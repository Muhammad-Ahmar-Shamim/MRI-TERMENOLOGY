Our Services

We offer a range of services to cater to your needs. Explore what we can do for you below:



Brain MRI

Used to diagnose conditions such as brain tumors, stroke, multiple sclerosis (MS), Alzheimer's disease, epilepsy, and infections.



Functional MRI (fMRI)

Measures and monitors brain activity. It helps in evaluating cognitive functions like memory, speech, and movement control.



Spinal Cord MRI:

Essential for assessing spinal cord injuries, herniated discs, tumors, and other spinal disorders.



Cerebral Angiography:

Uses MRI to view blood vessels in the brain to identify aneurysms, blockages, or other abnormalities.
